Staff Rules & Regulations
Code of Conduct
  • Maintain professional behavior and respect toward all complainants at all times.
  • Treat all complaints with equal importance regardless of the complainant's social status.
  • Avoid any form of discrimination, bias, or prejudice in complaint handling.
  • Maintain strict confidentiality of sensitive information provided by complainants.
  • Dress appropriately in official uniform or designated attire during duty hours.
Operational Procedures
  • Register all incoming complaints in the system within 24 hours of receipt.
  • Verify complaint details with supporting documents and evidence before marking as verified.
  • Provide updates on complaint status to the complainant within 48 hours.
  • Follow the investigation hierarchy: Register â†’ Verify â†’ Investigate â†’ Resolve.
  • Document all actions and remarks in the complaint record with accurate timestamps.
  • Escalate high-priority complaints (marked URGENT) to senior officers immediately.
Evidence & Documentation
  • Collect and preserve all physical and digital evidence according to legal standards.
  • Maintain a chain of custody for all evidence collected.
  • Upload supporting documents (ID proof, evidence photos, reports) to the system.
  • Ensure all evidence is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • Label all evidence with case ID, date, and officer name.
Data Security & Privacy
  • Never disclose your login credentials to anyone, including colleagues.
  • Change your password every 90 days or when requested by administration.
  • Log out from the system immediately after completing your work.
  • Do not access complaint records unless authorized for that case.
  • Report any suspicious activity or unauthorized access attempts immediately.
Prohibited Actions
  • Do not share confidential complaint information with unauthorized persons.
  • Do not accept bribes or gifts in exchange for favorable complaint treatment.
  • Do not manipulate or falsify complaint records and evidence.
  • Do not use the system for personal gain or non-official purposes.
  • Do not delay complaint processing without valid reason.
  • Violation of these rules may result in disciplinary action.

Acknowledgment: By using this system, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these rules and regulations. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
